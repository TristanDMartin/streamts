Our Story

We're on a mission to empower streamers with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed

How It All Started

Streamer Tips was born from a simple observation: too many talented creators were struggling to grow their audience and monetize their content effectively.

Our founder, a former professional streamer with over 10 years of experience, noticed that the streaming landscape was becoming increasingly competitive, but the resources available to help new creators were scattered and often outdated.

In 2020, we launched our platform with a simple mission: to provide streamers with the tools, knowledge, and community support they need to turn their passion into a sustainable career.

Our Values

These core principles guide everything we do at Streamer Tips

Empowerment

We believe everyone deserves the opportunity to turn their passion into a career. Our tools and resources are designed to level the playing field for creators of all backgrounds.

Community

Streaming is a journey best traveled with others. We foster a supportive community where creators can connect, collaborate, and grow together.

Innovation

The streaming landscape is constantly evolving. We stay ahead of trends and continuously develop new tools to help creators adapt and thrive.

Transparency

We believe in honest communication and providing creators with clear, actionable insights to help them make informed decisions about their content and career.
