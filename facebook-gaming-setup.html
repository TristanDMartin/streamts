How to Create a Facebook Gaming Account

Step-by-step guide to setting up your Facebook Gaming presence

Facebook Gaming is a great option for new streamers, especially if you already have a Facebook presence and want to grow your audience through your existing network.

Steps:

  1. Sign in to Facebook: Go to facebook.com and log in with your personal account. If you don't have one, create an account first.
  2. Create a Facebook Page:
    • Visit Facebook Pages.
    • Choose "Gaming Video Creator" as the category.
    • Give your Page a name (this can be your stream name or brand).
    • Add a profile picture and banner that represents your stream.
  3. Set up your Page:
    • Fill out the "About" section.
    • Add links to your other social platforms (like Twitch or YouTube).
    • Enable messaging if you want to connect directly with your audience.
  4. Connect Your Stream Software:
    • Open your streaming software (like OBS or Streamlabs).
    • Go to your Creator Studio: facebook.com/creatorstudio.
    • Under Live Dashboard, copy your Stream Key and Server URL.
    • Paste them into your streaming software settings.

Level Up Program (Optional)

Apply for the Level Up Program to unlock additional features:

  • Go to Facebook Gaming Creator Portal
  • Make sure you meet the requirements (like streaming gaming content regularly and having at least 100 followers on your page)
  • This program unlocks HD streaming, monetization tools, and more

Important Note

Keep your Stream Key private and never share it with anyone. This key is used to authenticate your streaming software with Facebook Gaming.
